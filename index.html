Visit Haugesund

Fun things to do in Haugesund

City Hall

Check out our fresh pink town hall in the middle of the city!

Jazz festival

In August you can experience a good time during the anual jazz festival

The library

Our infamous library just got renovated, check it out if you have some reading up to do

The home of the vikings

Monument to King Harald

Experience the monument to the famous King Harald who united Norway around the 800's. It's said that he resided around these parts!

Viking village

If you take a trip to Avaldsnes you can experience the viking village, a reconstructed longhouse like in the bronze age and a viking market.
